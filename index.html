Lima, Peru

Peru, located in South America, is celebrated for its breathtaking landscapes, from the Andes mountains to the lush Amazon rainforest. It's home to Machu Picchu, a famous Incan citadel and UNESCO World Heritage site. Peru's cuisine is diverse and world-renowned, featuring iconic dishes like *ceviche* (fresh fish marinated in citrus), *lomo saltado* (stir-fried beef), and hearty stews such as *aji de gallina*. This culinary diversity reflects a fusion of indigenous, Spanish, African, and Asian influences, making Peru a top destination for food lovers.
